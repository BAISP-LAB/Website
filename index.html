實驗室簡介 (Laboratory Introduction)


我們實驗室主要研究生醫訊號的處理與人工智慧間的結合，包括EEG、MEG、FMRI等訊號的分析與應用。具體研究方向涵蓋疾病預防、認知功能評估（如記憶力、注意力測試）、駕駛注意力監測等方面，以及神經影像技術的開發與應用。我們致力於通過先進的深度學習與機器學習技術，提升對腦電波和其他生理訊號的理解，推動智慧醫療的進步。

Our laboratory mainly studies the combination of medical signal processing and artificial intelligence, including the analysis and application of EEG, MEG, FMRI and other signals. Specific research directions include disease prevention, cognitive function assessment (such as memory and attention tests), driving attention monitoring, etc., as well as the development and application of neuroimaging technology. We are committed to improving the understanding of brain waves and other physiological signals through advanced deep learning and machine learning technologies to promote the advancement of smart medical care.

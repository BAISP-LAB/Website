實驗室簡介 (Laboratory Introduction)


我們的實驗室專注於開發智慧生醫技術，特別是針對神經訊號與醫學影像的分析工具。我們結合腦波(EEG)、功能性磁振造影 (fMRI)、心電圖(ECG)等生醫訊號與人工智慧技術，目標是研發能應用於臨床疾病的治療、監測、預防，以及提升日常生活品質的創新解決方案。
目前實驗室的研究項目涵蓋多個領域，包括：開發精準的神經調控療法並探討其生理機制、提升駕駛安全的系統、以及心血管手術後腦功能評估的技術。除了應用傳統的機器學習技術與最新的深度學習方法進行分類與預測，我們也注重模型的可解釋性，藉此建立神經生理的相關標記，推動對腦科學的更深入理解，並促進智慧醫療的進步。

Our laboratory focuses on the development of intelligent biomedical technologies, particularly in the creation of tools for analyzing neurophysiological signals and medical imaging. We integrate biomedical signals such as electroencephalography (EEG), functional magnetic resonance imaging (fMRI), and electrocardiography (ECG) with artificial intelligence technologies. Our goal is to develop innovative solutions that can be applied to the treatment, monitoring, and prevention of clinical diseases, as well as improving the quality of daily life.
The current research projects in our lab cover various fields, including the development of precise neuromodulation therapies and the exploration of their neurophysiological mechanisms, systems to enhance driving safety, and techniques for brain function assessment after cardiovascular surgery. In addition to applying traditional machine learning techniques and the latest deep learning methods for classification and prediction, we also emphasize model interpretability, aiming to establish relevant neurophysiological markers, further advancing our understanding of brain science, and promoting the progress of intelligent healthcare.
